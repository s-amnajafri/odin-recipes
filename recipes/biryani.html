Biryani

A delicious plate of homemade biryani

Biryani is a fragrant, celebratory South Asian mixed rice dish, originating from Persian and Mughal cuisine. Traditionally made using the dum pukht method, where the pot is sealed with dough to steam-cook the ingredients together, allowing the rice to absorb the meat juices.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice 3 cups
  • Chicken 1 kg
  • Yoghurt 1 cup
  • Ginger Garlic Paste 2 tsp
  • Lemon Juice 2-4 tsp
  • Onions 2-3
  • Tomatoes 2-3
  • Green Chillies 2
  • Coriander & Mint Leaves
  • Cloves 3-4
  • Oil 1/2 cup
